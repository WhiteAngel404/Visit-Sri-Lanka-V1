Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know before visiting

Best Time to Visit

December to March for west and south coasts. April to September for east coast and cultural triangle.

Learn More

Visa Requirements

Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) required for most visitors. Apply online before arrival.

Learn More

Currency & Costs

Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). Very affordable destination with great value for money.

Learn More

Getting Around

Trains, buses, tuk-tuks, and private drivers available. Train travel is scenic and affordable.
